\begin{recipe}{Hatch Green Chili}{20 minutes}{6 hours}
  \ing[1]{}{pork tenderloin, cut into thirds}
  \ing[1]{}{sweet onion}
  \ing[4]{}{jalapenos, diced}
  \ing[4]{}{cloves garlic, chopped}
\ing[32]{oz}{hatch green chile sauce or salsa verde}
\ing[2]{tbsp}{butter}
\ing[1]{tbsp}{coriander}
\ing[1\fr12]{tsp}{cumin}
\ing[1]{tsp}{cayenne pepper}
\ing[\fr12]{tsp}{paprika}
\ing{to taste}{Salt, pepper, sugar}

Combine ingredients in a large crock pot. Cook 6 hours on high or 8 hours on
low until pork melts. Shred pork. Finish with 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
and juice of 1 lime. Serve over rice, garnish with more cilantro
\end{recipe}
